medpy.utilities.argparseu.sequenceOfFloatsLt¶

medpy.utilities.argparseu.sequenceOfFloatsLt(string)[source]¶

A custom type for the argparse commandline parser. Accepts only colon-separated lists of valid float values that are less than 0.

Examples

>>> parser.add_argument('argname', type=sequenceOfFloatsLt, help='help')
